Personal Build — 2025

Holy
Venom.

Attack Surface Intelligence Platform for SMEs

  • Built a 9-module passive scanner covering SSL/TLS, HTTP headers, DNS intelligence, web application fingerprinting, email security, port scanning, subdomain enumeration (20k wordlist + CT logs), cloud asset discovery (S3/GCS/Azure/Firebase), and full OSINT (Shodan API, HIBP, typosquatting, Wayback Machine).
  • Engineered an intelligent scan agent that guides scan phases in real time, filters false positives based on evidence, adjusts severity, and correlates multiple vulnerabilities into exploitable attack chains.
  • Generated four report formats per scan tailored to different audiences: Executive (business language, financial impact), Technical (OWASP/MITRE mapped), Remediation Checklist, and Attack Chain narrative.
  • Validated against bbc.co.uk — 59 confirmed findings, zero false positives, including real sensitive subdomains, SPF misconfigurations, CSP weaknesses, CDN origin leaks, and typosquatting domains.
  • Architected three product modes: Scan (on-demand assessment), Monitor (weekly attack surface tracking with change detection), and Investigate (post-breach IOC detection and breach timeline hypothesis).
